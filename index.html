Design & Develop of Photomap
I work as a Design Director during the day, and in my free time I explore places that I've never been to with my camera. Ever since I started taking pictures, I found myself saving all my photos into my hard drive, grouping them by location or occasion. The idea of Photomap is simple: to give my amateur photography a home and to help me remember the places that I have been to in a more visual way. Making this site has also helped me practice my skills in web development.
